Delicious Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tablespoons melted butter

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Create a well in the center and pour in the milk, egg, and melted butter.
  3. Mix until smooth.
  4. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  5. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake.
  6. Cook until bubbles form and the edges are dry.
  7. Flip and cook until browned on the other side.
  8. Serve hot with your favorite toppings!

Enjoy your pancakes!

These fluffy pancakes are perfect for a delicious breakfast or brunch. Serve them with maple syrup, fresh berries, or whipped cream for an extra treat!
